What is it like to be a Computer Science Student?

Computer science is an amazing field filled with a variety of concentrations, people, and opportunties. Hear from some students from Oklahoma Christian Univeristy about why they chose to major in computer science:

“I get to learn how to tell computers what to do!”

-Lance Skinner, Senior Undergraduate Computer Science & Graduate Cybersecurity Student

“Being a computer science student is fun because you continuously learn new things about how software works. Something about the Internet seems so easy to use, but it is incredibly complex underneath the surface. I find it engaging."

- John Every, Senior Undergraduate Computer Science & Graduate Data Science Student

“I chose computer science because I love the idea of being able to create something out of nothing, whether that be through web dev, mob dev, ss, there is nothing more rewarding than bringing someone's idea to life."

- Gus Cordero, Senior Undergraduate Computer Science with Gaming & Animation, & Graduate AI Student

“I am a computer scientist because I love problem solving. I get to have a job where all I do is solve problems and be creative, what more could I want!”

-Emily Forster, Senior Undergraduate Computer Science with Cybersecurity Student
