How to Prepare

It's not easy to be a computer science student! A lot of skills are needed. Some of these may be new, but you might be surprised how many skills you already know!

As with all skills, you can improve by practicing and gaining experience. All sorts of skills are useful, so find what you like doing, and work on it!

Broadly speaking, skills are divided into Hard Skills and Soft Skills.

In this case when we talk about hard skills, we mean things related to the direct practice of computer science - working with technology directly.

Soft skills are other skills that a computer scientist will regularly use outside of their work with technologies. It also includes skills that are hard to quantitatively prove.

To prepare to be a computer scientist, look at the skills you'll need, and start practicing! The more that interest you, and the more you practice them, the better prepared you'll be!
